THIS PAGE SHOWS THE FOOTBALL SCHOLARSHIPS PROVIDED BY GOVERNMENT OF INDIA

Given below are the conditions and links to the respective scholarships

  1. NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF SPORTS DEVELOPMENT

    2. THE NISD currently offers scholarships to 20 footballers every year.The seleced athletes receive between Rs 6,000($72) to Rs 9,000 ($110) per month.

    ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
    (a)The age of the trainee should be between 8 to 21 years.
    (b)The trainee to provide a valid and approved zonal,state,or national competition certificate.
    (c)The trainee should not be registered to other sports scholarships.

  2. INDIAN OIL SPORTS SCHOLARSHIPS

    3. The IOS offers scholarships to 23 footballers annually.The selected trainee receive between Rs 1,80,000($2161.73) to Rs 3,60,000($4323.46) per annum.

    ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
    (a) The age of the trainee should be between 13 to 19 years.
    (b) The trainee should have a satisfactory resume.
    (c)The trainee should meet the stated health conditions.

  3. AIRPORT AUTHORITY OF INDIA

    4. The AAI offers scholarships to footballers above 14 years of age.The selected trainees receive Rs 12,000($144) and the trainees above 18 years receive Rs16000($192.14)

    ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
    (a)The age of the trainee should not exceed 25 years.
    (b) Trainees must have competed in atleast Junior,Senior,National ,All India University and National School games.
    (c)The trainee should provide the selection committee with proper age related documents.
